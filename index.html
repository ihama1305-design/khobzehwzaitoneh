Our Story

Bread, olive oil, and the language of hospitality

Khobzeh w Zaitoneh means a piece of bread and an olive. It is a simple Palestinian image, but it carries a full table: warm bread passed from hand to hand, olive oil poured generously, and dishes remembered from home.

In Abu Dhabi, that feeling becomes a modern Palestinian table shaped by fresh dough, bright salads, slow-cooked daily dishes, oven pastries, sumac, zaatar, herbs, and the comfort of family-style food.
