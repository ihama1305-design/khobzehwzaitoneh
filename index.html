Our Story

Bread, olive oil, and the language of hospitality

Khobzeh w Zaitoneh means a piece of bread and an olive. It is a simple Palestinian image, but it carries a full table: the bread passed warm from hand to hand, the olive oil poured generously, and the dishes remembered from home.

In Abu Dhabi, the restaurant brings that feeling into a modern, welcoming space. Expect fresh dough, bright salads, slow-cooked daily dishes, pastries from the oven, and flavours built around olive oil, herbs, sumac, zaatar, and family-style comfort.
